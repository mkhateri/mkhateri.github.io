Mohammad Khateri

Final-year PhD candidate working on brain ultrastructure analysis, simulation, super-resolution, and segmentation.

I am a last-year PhD candidate working on medical computer vision and computational imaging, with a focus on biomedia and medical imaging. My doctoral thesis, “Computational Methods for Brain Ultrastructure Analysis: Simulation, Super-Resolution, and Segmentation”, is in its final stages and expected to be submitted by the end of February 2026.

I completed a six-month research visit at Harvard Medical School in Boston, USA, and continue close collaboration. My research spans electron microscopy (EM), magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), ultrasound, and biomedical imaging.
